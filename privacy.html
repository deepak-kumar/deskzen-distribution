Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2024

At DeskZen, we believe your data belongs to you. This Privacy Policy describes how DeskZen ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the DeskZen macOS application.

1. Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data.**

  • We do not track how you use the app.
  • We do not collect your IP address or device identifiers.
  • We do not have access to your files, documents, or desktop content.
  • We use no third-party analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase).

2. App Permissions

DeskZen requests specific system permissions solely to perform its core functionality locally on your Mac:

  • Automation (Apple Events): This permission is required to tell macOS Finder to hide/show desktop icons and to tell System Events to toggle Do Not Disturb mode. This communication happens strictly between the DeskZen app and your local system components. No data leaves your device.

3. Managing Your Data

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data for you to review, download, or delete from our servers.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the date at the top of this policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@deskzen.app.
